Edge Cases

  • No currency here — just the number 1,234 by itself.
  • Already converted: ¥500 (should not double-convert on reload)
  • Large number: ¥1,000,000,000
  • Decimal: ¥99.50
  • Zero: ¥0

Dynamic Content

Click the button to inject new content. The MutationObserver should convert it automatically.
